CHECKERS

From the depths of the underground comes Checkers, a dedicated DJ/producer with a wide spectrum of music intertwining through multiple genres. Checkers intricately weaves the balance between reading a crowd and playing music that inspires and drives him personally.

To date, Checkers has played a robust span of highly acclaimed festivals and venues with highlights including Electric Daisy Carnival (Las Vegas), Circuit of the Americas (Austin), and has played along side names like Carl Cox and Croatia Squad.

Whether it's rocking a warehouse, a high-profile venue, or a festival Checkers brings his finely tuned appreciation for exceptional music that moves your soul each and every time he touches the decks. His dedication and devotion to curating an uplifting and inspirational vibe shines a light on the unwavering passion that drives his craft.
